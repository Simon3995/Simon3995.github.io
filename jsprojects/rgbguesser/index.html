Try to guess the hex (hexadecimal) format of the color you see, and you will see how close you are to the correct answer.

This RGB color guessing game uses the DeltaE 2000 formula to determine color similarity.

A DeltaE value of less than 1 (or a similarity above 99%) should be imperceptible to the human eye, and will thus lead to a "perfect" rating.
